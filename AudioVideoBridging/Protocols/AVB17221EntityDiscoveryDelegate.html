Overview

@protocol   AVB17221EntityDiscoveryDelegate

AVB17221EntityDiscoveryDelegate is an objective-c protocol for classes acting as the discovery delegate of the AVB17221EntityDiscovery object. There is a separate callback for each phase of entity discover and property updates. Callbacks are separated between entities discovered on the network and those published locally on the Mac.
