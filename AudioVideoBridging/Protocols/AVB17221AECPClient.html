AVB17221AECPClient Protocol Reference

Declared in AVB17221AECPInterface.h

Overview

@protocol AVB17221AECPClient

AVB17221AECPClient is an objective-c protocol for classes intending to receive callbacks from an AVB1722AECPInterface instance. Commands and responses are provided with separate callbacks.
