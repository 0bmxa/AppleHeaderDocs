@class SCNLevelOfDetail

+ levelOfDetailWithGeometry:screenSpaceRadius:

This is a convenience method to create a level of detail with a coverage radius threshold mode.

+ (instancetype)levelOfDetailWithGeometry:(nullable SCNGeometry *)geometry screenSpaceRadius:(CGFloat)radius

Parameters

geometry

The geometry for this level of detail. nil is supported and indicates that no geometry should be rendered for this level of detail.
radius

The maximum radius in screen-space that this level of detail is suitable for. The coverage radius is calculated from the projected bounding sphere and expressed in pixels.
