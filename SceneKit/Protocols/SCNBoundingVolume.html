SCNBoundingVolume Protocol Reference

Conforms to NSObject
Declared in SCNBoundingVolume.h

Overview

@protocol SCNBoundingVolume

You can currently use this protocol to get the bounding boxes of nodes and geometries.

– getBoundingBoxMin:max: required method

Fill the min and max vectors with the min and max vertex of the bounding box.

- (BOOL)getBoundingBoxMin:(nullable SCNVector3 *)min max:(nullable SCNVector3 *)max

Parameters

min

A pointer to a SCNVector3 to store the min vertex of the bounding box into.
max

A pointer to a SCNVector3 to store the max vertex of the bounding box into.

Discussion

@method getBoundingBoxMin:max:

the returned bounding box is in local space of the receiver.

Declared In

SCNBoundingVolume.h

– setBoundingBoxMin:max: required method

Override the receiver bounding box with the min and max vectors provided (in local space of the receiver).

- (void)setBoundingBoxMin:(nullable SCNVector3 *)min max:(nullable SCNVector3 *)max

Parameters

min

A pointer to a SCNVector3 representing the min vertex of the desired bounding box.
max

A pointer to a SCNVector3 representing the max vertex of the desired bounding box.

Discussion

@method setBoundingBoxMin:max:

Passing nil as arguments will recompute the original bounding box of the receiver.
