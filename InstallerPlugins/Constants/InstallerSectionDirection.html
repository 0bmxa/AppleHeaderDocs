InstallerSectionDirection

@enum InstallerSectionDirection

Describes the direction in which a pane is entered or exited. @constant InstallerDirectionForward Specifies that the pane was entered/exited while moving in the forward direction. @constant InstallerDirectionBackward Specifies that the pane was entered/exited while moving backwards. @constant InstallerDirectionUndefined Specifies that the pane was entered/exited using the gotoPane: method. Since the pane is “jumped” to rather than moved to going forward or back, InstallerDirectionUndefined is used.
