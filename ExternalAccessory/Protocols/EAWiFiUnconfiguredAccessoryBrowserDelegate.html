@protocol EAWiFiUnconfiguredAccessoryBrowserDelegate

The delegate of a EAWiFiUnconfiguredAccessoryBrowser object must adopt the EAWiFiUnconfiguredAccessoryBrowserDelegate protocol. The required callbacks keep the delegate informed of the state of the search and configuration processes.
